Project Description — Prism Insurance Pvt. Ltd (Power BI Dashboard)

Project Overview
Designed and developed an end-to-end interactive Power BI dashboard for Prism Insurance Pvt. Ltd
to monitor and analyze the company's insurance business operations, including premium collection, coverage exposure, claims processing, and customer segmentation. 
The dashboard transforms raw transactional data into meaningful visual insights, empowering stakeholders to make data-driven decisions with speed and clarity.

Data Handling & Modeling
- Performed data cleaning, transformation, and shaping using Power Query Editor
- Established relationships between tables using a proper data model (Star/Snowflake schema)
- Created DAX measures for aggregated KPIs such as Total Premium, Total Claims, and Coverage summation
- Applied appropriate data types, formatting, and number scaling
  
 Business Insights Delivered
- Travel insurance is the most profitable product line by premium volume
- Adults are the highest-risk demographic segment in terms of claim value
- The claim rejection rate is significantly high (~43%)**, suggesting a need to review underwriting policies or improve customer claim guidance
- The customer base is gender-balanced, indicating no demographic bias in policy distribution
- Over ₹13.6 Cr in claims remain pending, pointing towards potential processing delays that may impact customer satisfaction

Outcome
The dashboard serves as a **strategic decision-support tool** for the leadership and operations teams, 
enabling them to identify revenue trends, monitor claims lifecycle, optimize product strategy, and 
improve overall operational efficiency across the insurance value chain.
